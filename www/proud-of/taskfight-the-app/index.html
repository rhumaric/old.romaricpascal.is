TaskFight – The app

TaskFight is a personal app project to help sort task priorities, for which I did both the design of the branding and the development of the application.

Building TaskFight was mostly the occasion to keep my skills up to date by trying out new tools (namely ReactJS and Redux) in the context of making a real application.
