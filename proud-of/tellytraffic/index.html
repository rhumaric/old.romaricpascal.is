TellyTraffic

For TellyTraffic, I helped tallhatDesign convert the Flash website of their client to a more modern & responsive HTML/CSS/Javascript one.

The main feature of the design was the smooth scrolling navigation. Its different sections are not stacked one above each other, but spread in different direction around the home section. For narrow screens, the design would reduce to a more classic “one column” layout.
